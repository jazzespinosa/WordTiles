How To Play

Guess the word in limited turns!

1

Make a Guess

Type any valid word with the correct number of letters and press Enter to submit.

2

Check the Colors

After each guess, the tiles will change color to show how close your guess was.

3

Keep Guessing

Use the feedback to refine your guesses. Solve the word before you run out of turns!

Color Guide

A

Correct

The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.

B

Present

The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.

C

Incorrect

The letter is not in the word at all.
